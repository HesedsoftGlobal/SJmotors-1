Products

Ball Bearing

Ball bearings are meant for high speed activities such as motors. It comes in few different variations It comes in two cover types Rubber and Steel ZZ and 2Rsr,DD, Luu. C3, C4

Roller Bearing

Roller bearings are meant to bear heavy loads and high durability. It comes in few variations N type, NF type, NJ type, NU type.

Tapered Roller Bearing

Tappered Roller Bearings are used to bear heavy loads.Most commonly used for all purposes

Spherical Roller Bearing

Spherical Roller Bearings are used to bear heavy loads and in places where it wants to be adjusted when something goes wrong, these bearings are highly durable. comes in 2 variation K and cc which states used with or without adapter.

Spherical Roller Thrust Bearing

Spherical Roller Thrust Bearings are used to bear heavy loads and mainly used for loads in a thrusting position in an upright angle.

Thrust Ball Bearing

Thrust Ball Bearings are used to bear heavy loads from a vertical position and used get High speeds .

Double Row Ball Bearing

Double Row Ball Bearings are used to bear heavy loads and used get High speeds .

Double Row Self-Align Ball Bearing

Double Row Ball Bearings are used to bear heavy loads and this bearing aligns its self for adjustments to prevent failure, used get High speeds .

Plain Bearing(Eyeball bearing)

Plain Bearings are used for alignments and adjustment areas to obtain smooth adjustments.

Pillow Block Bearing

Pillow block bearings are used commonly in machinary, which comes with a housing to keep in place the bearing. It has many variations UCP, UCF, UCT, UCPH, UCFC, UCEH, UCFL and more..

Linear Bearing

Linear Bearings are used for linear movement along a shaft to obtain smooth movement lower the friction.
